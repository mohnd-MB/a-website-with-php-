Echitectural Engineering

Architectural engineering, also known as building engineering, is an engineering discipline that deals with the technological aspects and interdisciplinary approach to planning, designing, constructing and operating buildings, such as analysis and integrated design of environmental systems (energy conservation, HVAC, plumbing, lighting, fire protection, acoustics, vertical and horizontal transport), structural systems, behavior and properties of building components and materials, and construction management
